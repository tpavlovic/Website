LOCATED IN VRAČAR, BELGRADE

Situated in Neimar, close to a park, Voulez-Vous captures you and leaves breathless with its open space, garden component, its brightness, openness and friendly atmosphere. At the second glance it will conquer you with its specialized cuisine and the dishes prepared with devotion and love by our chefs.

Altogether you can sense the balance of warmth and friendly atmosphere combined with excellent cuisine and cozy environment, wrapped in excellent prices. A safe haven in the city for those for whom combining lifestyle with its business is essential, Voulez-Vous is equipped with WI-FI connection.
